Kings Way Schools were founded on February 5th, 2001, by Mr. Frank Muramura and Mrs. Loy Muramura with a clear, vital vision: to give an excellent, all-round education to students while deeply rooting them in academic excellence and strong moral values. The schools draws inspiration for its name from the historical route traveled along the Entebbe Road corridor by the Kabaka of Buganda.

Starting with just 55 students and 10 dedicated teachers, their profound commitment to holistic educational development transformed a humble dream into a multi-campus institution that now nurtures thousands of students.

Under their continuous leadership, the institution builds self-reliant, disciplined, and innovative young leaders fully prepared to navigate modern world challenges. The primary school campus operates as a mixed day and boarding school following the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) curriculum.

Welcome to Kings Way Schools

We offer a comprehensive range of educational services designed to unlock your child's full potential. Under the Kings Way umbrella, we operate three distinct institutions: a Nursery, Primary, and High school. This allows us to provide a seamless, all-round education that thoroughly prepares students for the future. Today, Kings Way Schools has grown into a leading educational institution in Uganda, proudly welcoming families from both Uganda and other countries. Your child’s future is our priority. With our experienced teaching staff, modern facilities, and commitment to holistic development, we provide the perfect environment for students to thrive. Whether you are based locally in Uganda or joining us from any of the other countries, you can trust that your child’s education is in the safest, most capable hands.

Nursery School (Ages 3 - 6)

At Kings Way Nursery School, we establish a joyful and nurturing early educational ecosystem. Utilizing modern early-learning frameworks, we cultivate essential cognitive, physical, and foundational social masteries.

Core Developmental Modules
  • Literacy & Language Acquisition Development
  • Numeracy Foundations & Spatial Reasoning

  • Fine & Gross Motor Coordination Skills

Baby Class

Focusing on basic socialization, language adaptation, shapes, and structural orientation patterns.

Middle Class

Advancing toward early phonetic letter tracing, fundamental drawing assignments, and number tracing.

Top Class

Honing robust pre-reading sentences, mathematical equations, and core primary preparation modules.

At Kings Way Primary School, we combine strict national curriculum standards with supportive, child-centered instructional methods. Our academic path guarantees complete mastery of foundational subjects, thoroughly preparing pupils for competitive corporate futures and exceptional PLE results.

Code of Conduct

At Kings Way Schools, we strongly believe in moulding self-reliant, morally upright, and law-abiding future leaders. Click any rule to read more details.

All students must strictly report to class by 7:30 AM every morning. Attendance of early lessons, prep classes, and weekly school assemblies is compulsory. Latecomers will face disciplinary sessions.

English remains the strictly enforced language of instruction and general communication on campus. Peer interaction must be respectful, peaceful, and support the mixed school co-existence principles.

Personal smartphones, tablet devices, and mobile phones are strictly prohibited within the boarding houses and classrooms. Computer literacy training is strictly undertaken inside our supervised ICT laboratory suites.

We maintain zero tolerance for bullying, substance abuse, and exam malpractices. Active participation in spiritual activities and spiritual counseling services is strongly encouraged to cultivate God-fearing souls.
